About

The mission

I'm on a mission to improve the state of human health. Let me be clear, I'm proud of how far we've come so far in the health sciences and I want to acknowledge the enormous efforts of all healthcare workers. The current system nevertheless fails to adress the overwhelming increase in various diseases and their impact on quality of life and longevity. It is time for a change. I plan to establish a new type of health institute that focuses on thorough evaluation of health state, interventions to strengthen health and to prevent the onset of disease.

The plan

The earnings from NFT projects and trades will be used to fund a health d'app on the blockchain: Solana Health. I create my own NFT art that is meant to raise awareness of physical and mental health in various ways. I will also callaborate with artists, empowering them in the process, and trade NFT collectibles. By interacting with my NFT projects and collectibles, and by spreading the word, you will help to fund the development of this new d'app. Solana Health will be a platform dedicated to health improvement. You can learn more on the website.
