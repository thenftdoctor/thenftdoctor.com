About

The mission

I'm on a mission to improve the state of human health.
Let me be clear, I'm proud of how far we've come so far in the health sciences and I want to acknowledge the enormous efforts of all healthcare workers. The current system nevertheless fails to adress the overwhelming increase in various diseases and their impact on quality of life and longevity. It is time for a change. I plan to establish a new type of health institute that focuses on thorough evaluation of health state, interventions to strengthen health and to prevent the onset of disease.

The plan

The earnings from NFT projects and trades will be used to fund the new health institute. I will create my own NFT art projects (to be announced on this website) that will raise awareness of physical and mental health in various ways. I will also trade NFT collectibles, empowering artists in the process. So by interacting with my NFT projects and collectibles, and by spreading the word, you will help to fund the new health institute. The current plan is to bootstrap the health institute as a digital blockchain platform, where healthy behaviours are rewarded with $health tokens that can be used to pay for similar products and services. We might later build a physical location that will take this concept to a new level.
